Who are we?

We are PSU's first ever debate club! We meet every week to debate about various issues in the world and hone our argumentative speaking skills. Join us and battle in the war of words.

Location: SCR-101, Building 101, PSU Male Campus

Time: 12 - 1 PM, every Tuesday
