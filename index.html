Our Story

a little bit about the club

The Prince Sultan University Debate Club is an interactive hub in which students are given a platform to argumentatively voice their opinions in a friendly-competitive environment.

The Debate Club at Prince Sultan University aims to offer people with an argumentative spirit a platform to defend their beliefs and enrich their knowledge. contact us for more info.

Why join us

Knowledge

Debating gives you a useful platform to share your knowledge with others and fighting for what you believe is right. You can also use this as an opportunity to learn new information from opposing views.
